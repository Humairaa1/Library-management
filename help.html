Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Have Questions? Here you'll find the answer.

How do I register for library membership?

You can register by filling out the membership form at the library or through our online portal.

How many books can I borrow at once?

Students can borrow up to 5 books, while faculty members can borrow up to 10 books.

What happens if I return a book late?

Late returns will incur a fine of 50 TK per day per book.

How do I find a book in the library?

You can use the online catalog on our website to search for books by title, author, or subject. Physical copies are arranged by category on the shelves.

Can I suggest new books for the library?

Yes! You can submit book recommendations through the library suggestion form or email us at library@university.com.
