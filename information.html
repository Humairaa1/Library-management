Collections

The university library offers a diverse collection of books, journals, and digital resources across multiple disciplines. Students and faculty can access a vast range of materials, including textbooks, research papers, and reference books tailored to their fields of study. The CSE department has books on programming, data structures, artificial intelligence, and networking, while the BBA section includes materials on finance, marketing, management, and entrepreneurship. The Civil Engineering collection covers structural design, construction management, and environmental engineering, whereas the EEE section provides resources on circuits, power systems, and electronics. Additionally, the English department features literature, linguistics, and literary analysis books, and the Islamic Studies section contains Quranic studies, Islamic history, and jurisprudence. The library also offers access to digital resources, research papers, and academic journals, ensuring comprehensive support for learning and research.

Library Membership

To borrow books and access library resources, students and faculty must first register as library members. Membership grants access to a wide collection of academic materials, including textbooks, research papers, and digital resources.

Membership Terms & Conditions:

  • Eligibility: All university students, faculty, and staff can apply for membership.
  • Registration: Members must complete a registration form and provide a valid university ID.
  • Registration: Members must complete a registration form and provide a valid university ID.
  • Loan Limits: A member can borrow up to 5 books at a time for a period of 15 days.
  • Renewals & Returns: Books can be renewed once if not requested by another member. Late returns may result in fines.
  • Lost or Damaged Books: Members are responsible for replacing lost or damaged books or paying the applicable fine.
  • Library Conduct: Silence must be maintained, and all resources must be used responsibly.
